Research Highlights

PLFS Employment Analysis

Large-scale empirical work using PLFS microdata reveals persistent gender gaps and rural–urban differentials in India’s labour market.

  • Analyzed 200,000+ PLFS survey responses
  • Identified a 23% employment gap between rural and urban women
  • Built ML models predicting employment probability with 87% accuracy
  • Interactive dashboards adopted by 3 state governments
